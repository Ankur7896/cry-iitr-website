About Us

  • April 2012

    Our Humble Beginnings

    CRY IIT Roorkee Chapter began with a few students studying in IIT Roorkee.

  • Who We Are ?

    We are a group of volunteers currently studying in IIT Roorkee working to improve the lives of under privileged children in our society, especially around Roorkee.

  • What We Do ?

    Our basic aim is to educate each and every under privileged child in or around Roorkee. For that we conduct surveys to identify the children who need help, get children admitted to schools through RTE, work towards ensuring them a good environment to live and grow, help them in studies through mentorship and Work to create a more aware society in a whole.

  • How We Do ?

    Each volunteer takes out time from studies and works towards the cause. We get help from the students and the professors in the campus who come forward to support us in our initiatives. We also set up stalls during fests to raise awareness for this great cause.
